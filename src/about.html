About us

Founded in 2020 by adventure‑driven duo Maya Alvarez and Luca Bianchi, Van Travel began as a shared notebook of road‑trip ideas that quickly turned into a reality on four wheels. Maya, a former graphic designer from Barcelona, fell in love with the freedom of nomadic living after a solo week‑long trek through the Pyrenees, where she discovered that a well‑designed, compact space could become a moving canvas for creativity and comfort. Luca, an ex‑mechanic and sustainability engineer from Milan, spent his early career fine‑tuning high‑performance engines before realizing his true passion lay in building eco‑friendly mobile homes that minimize environmental impact. Together they combined Maya’s eye for aesthetic, seamless interiors with Luca’s technical expertise, converting a vintage Sprinter into the prototype that would launch Van Travel’s first curated fleet of fully equipped, solar‑powered vans.

 

Today, Van Travel is a trusted hub for modern explorers seeking hassle‑free, off‑grid adventures across Europe and beyond. We provide detailed van‑tour videos, customizable layouts, and a vibrant community where travelers share routes, campsite tips, and DIY upgrades. Our mission is to empower anyone to trade static living for boundless horizons, all while championing responsible travel and low‑impact design. Whether you’re a weekend wanderer or a full‑time road‑tripper, Van Travel equips you with the tools, inspiration, and support needed to turn the open road into your living room. Join us and experience the world from the comfort of your own mobile sanctuary.
